Solutions

In the digital age, we are surrounded by screens all the time. There is no getting away with not using technology. However, eye strain and vision problems that come with the constant use of screens is becoming a significant problem in the lives of people.

This has come with multiple ways to limit the over usage of screens. This includes the 20-20-20 tactic: after each 20 minutes step away from a screen for 20 seconds to look at objects 20 meters away. This allows the eyes to take breaks from staring intensely at an object at a close distance, like a screen

These habits are sometimes not well maintained. With our generation of shorter attention spans, what should they do in these 20 seconds? With OptimEye, we not only enforce these healthy habits, but also give users wellness activities, all while supporting women around the world by showcasing their works.
